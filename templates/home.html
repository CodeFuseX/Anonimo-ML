About Anonimo


Why Anonimo ?

This platform is for people to connect with each other and express themselves according to their convenience. The lockdown and quarantine have made us isolated from the entire world, so through this platform, we are trying to bridge that gap. This website is created to drive open conversations among their target group around mental health. So that they know they’re not the only people going through the hardships of the COVID-19 pandemic.

  • Anonymous Sharing of thoughts
  • Interaction with other users with similar views
  • Blogs and resources provided by our admins
  • Posting thoughts and suggestions
  • Interative comments
